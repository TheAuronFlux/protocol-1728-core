## üìê Core Architecture (The Logic)

The system operates under a **Lexicographical Order of Importance**. The user must not sacrifice a higher-tier area to optimize a lower-tier one. This prevents "fragile optimization."

### The 12 Domains (Hierarchy v1.0)
1.  **Health (Bio-Performance):** The biological engine. Without ATP/Sleep, cognitive execution is impossible.
2.  **Resilience & Risk (OPSEC):** Safety and continuity. Preventing "Game Over" scenarios.
3.  **Finance (Resources):** Runway and viability.
4.  **Direction (Problem Selection):** Choosing the right game before playing it well.
5.  **Knowledge (Epistemology):** Navigation and correct premises.
6.  **Capacity Management:** Matching load with energy.
7.  **Data & Metrics (Telemetry):** The "Go/No-Go" decision systems.
8.  **Psychosocial:** Network, feedback loops, and mental hygiene.
9.  **Productivity (Throughput):** Transforming direction into deliverables.
10. **Distribution:** Value delivery and feedback latency.
11. **Creativity (R&D):** Exploration of new value vectors.
12. **Moat & Reputation:** Compounding long-term advantages.
